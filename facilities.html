Our Facilities

State-of-the-art bakery kitchen, a dedicated cake design studio, and a full event planning workspace — all designed to bring your vision to life.

Fully Equipped Baking Kitchen

Our commercial kitchen includes convection ovens, deck ovens, heavy-duty planetary mixers, proofing cabinets, and a separate pastry bench for delicate work. Equipment is maintained and calibrated regularly to ensure consistent results.

  • Industrial ovens & mixers
  • Dedicated pastry and bread sections
  • Temperature-controlled storage
  • Allergen-aware prep zones

Cake Decorating & Design Studio

Our design studio is where custom cakes and showpieces are imagined and assembled. It’s equipped with turntables, airbrush stations, fondant tools, and a photography corner for final-quality checks.

  • Dedicated decorator benches
  • Airbrush and sugarcraft tools
  • Photography corner for quality checks

Storage & Preparation Areas

We maintain clean, temperature-controlled storage for ingredients and finished goods, plus organized prep areas for event décor and catering assembly.

  • Cold storage and dry goods room
  • Labelled & organized shelving
  • Separate prep lines for dietary requirements

Delivery & Logistics

Our delivery fleet uses insulated carriers and secure packaging to ensure items arrive on time and in perfect condition. For large events we coordinate timelines, on-site setup, and vendor logistics.

  • Insulated delivery vehicles
  • On-site setup teams for weddings & corporate events
  • Vendor coordination and timeline management

Hygiene & Quality Standards

We adhere to strict food-safety practices, regular staff training, and routine sanitation. All facilities are insured and inspected according to local regulations.
